We have several intuitive and easy-to-use example applications that can be used to get your Redux app up and running with Back&. Choose the starter that is most appropriate for you, tie it into your application, and get coding!

Connecting with Back&

To connect an example application with Back&, modify the backand.init to include your app's token and name data as follows:


    backand.init && backand.init({
      appName: '{{vm.currentApp.Name || 'Your-App-Name'}}',
      signUpToken: '{{vm.keys.signup || 'Your-SignUp-Token'}}',
      anonymousToken: '{{vm.keys.anonymous || 'Your-Anonymous-Token'}}',
      runSocket: true
    });
