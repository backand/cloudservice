Bringing Your Own Database


The Model diagram serves as a powerful UI for your app's data model, but it can provide a limit to the changes it is possible to make - particularly in some more advanced use cases. In recognition of this, Backand gives you the opportunity to make changes directly in your database using a third-party database modification tool. Once you've made your changes, simply click "Sync with Database" to import your changes - Backand will analyze your database's structure, and will make the corresponding updates to your app's data schema.
In order to make changes you can use any MySQL tools like these:
  • Workbench
  • phpMyAdmin
  • Sequel Pro
  • HeidiSQL

    • To connect your database, copy the details from the Database section of your app's security settings.
    After finalizing the changes, click on Sync With Database to import the changes into your app.
