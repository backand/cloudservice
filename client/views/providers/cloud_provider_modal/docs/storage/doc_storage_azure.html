Azure Storage account access

To get your Azure Storage 'Connection String' do this:

  • Sign in to the Azure portal.
  • In the Azure portal, expand the menu on the left side to open the menu of services, and choose More Services. Then, scroll down to Storage, and choose Storage accounts.
  • Navigate to your storage account, scroll down to Settings and then click Access keys to copy the CONNECTION STRING.
